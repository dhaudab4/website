About

The Modern School was established from a single room with only three student, which was started by the Mr Sidibe in 2018 the Director as a English training program. Since we began, we have built a network of more than 200 student spread over more than 50 nations.
Through a combination of standard learning, we enable our students to be able to speak standard english in order for them to  begin an English university degree course  by engaging them in intensive english training   Our qualifications are listed on the National Accreditation Board (NAB) in Ghana, and we are governed by the Office of Qualifications
